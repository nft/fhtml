How does Driftwatch detect schema drift?

Driftwatch snapshots your warehouse schemas on every run and diffs them against the contracts your dbt models declare. When a column changes type, disappears, or picks up unexpected nulls, we open an incident before your dashboards break.

Which warehouses do you support?

Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, Databricks, and Postgres today. ClickHouse support is in private beta — join the waitlist from your workspace settings.

Does Driftwatch read my actual data?

No. We query metadata tables and aggregate statistics only — row counts, null ratios, and type information. Raw rows never leave your warehouse, and you can review every query we run in the audit log.
